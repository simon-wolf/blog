Org is an Emacs major mode which is designed for note-taking and task management but it is also an authoring and publishing system, including support for working with source code. These notes are both my personal aid to remembering things whilst I become familiar with it but they may also be helpful for others who are new to Emacs and Org and want to dig into it.
